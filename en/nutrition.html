[Nutrition Volunteer Campaign 2024]✨

Hello Nutrifans!👋

Do you know about the importance of optimizing 1000 HPK in preventing stunting? 🤔
Optimizing a child's 1000 HPK is very important, starting from early pregnancy to 2 years after birth. At this time, stunting can be prevented by fulfilling nutrition. At 6 months the first can be achieved by providing good and exclusive breastfeeding. Then after 6-2 years breast milk can be supplemented with complementary foods or MPASI. Apart from that, stunting can be prevented by optimizing visible food delivery from the quality of the food, whether it meets balanced and varied nutrition or not.

For further details, friends, you can immediately read the material explained below the post above! Don't forget to share this post and share your knowledge with friends around yaaa! stay healthy guys!🤩
